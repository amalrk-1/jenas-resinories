Welcome to Jena's Resinories — a passionate home-based resin art studio nestled in Mylankoodal, Ilavalai, Jaffna. Every piece we create blends artistry, emotion, and craftsmanship into timeless resin treasures.

From personalized memory frames and custom keychains to beautiful figurines and photo gifts — each creation is made with love and delivered with care. Perfect for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries and every special moment worth remembering!
